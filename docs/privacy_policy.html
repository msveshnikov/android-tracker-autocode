1. Introduction

Steps Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information Collection

Steps Tracker does not require sign-in and does not collect or share personal data. The app uses your device's built-in sensors to count steps and calculate calories burned, distance walked, and time spent walking.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. You have the option to back up your data to your personal Google Drive account, but this is entirely under your control.

4. Third-Party Services

If you choose to sync your data with Samsung Health or Google Fit, please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
